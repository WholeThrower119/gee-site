On Screen Overlay - Gee-Watch Provides an onscreen display to show you what hotkeys do what, Aswell as player name and Modder statuses.. Inorder to see the overlay, you must highlight (select) a target. To do so, you just aim at them with right click (Gun is optional).

Configurable Hotkeys - Each Function of Gee-Watch can be bound to a different key. Instead of X to explode a player, you xan set it to E ot G. Whichever you prefer and like. You can also disable hotkeys that you dont like.

Control at each fingertip - Once you have the bounds set-up, you will be able to control people just by looking at them. Make the session yours.

Gee-Map is an idea from another developer that didnt want to finish it. Well, Gee-Man finished it and made it into a reality. This feature solves the problem of having to use the pause menu for any map related functions.

  • On screen map with a push of a button, like M, Tilde (~), F1-f8, etc. Quickly teleport with a left click or set a waypoint with number 1.
  • Zoom Support by default with Scroll or Shift. Makes it easier to see what your doing.
  • Remote Explosion support built in. You can orbital strike any location, from any location.
  • Map Blips/Icons Supported by default. See players, Collectibles, Mission markers and more right from within Gee-Map.

Special Features

Gee-Skid's features are designed to help you improve your experiance & enjoyment with GTA Modding.
This script expandes the functionality of the Popular 2Take1 Mod menu.

  • Gee-Watch

    Enables 'Watch-Dogs Like' World hacking.
    Works on NPC's & Players.
    Aim at them and push a hotkey such Explode, Launch, hijack, etc. All keys are configurable.

  • Trolls & Attacks

    Many trolling features, such as Flare spam, BMX Slap, Kamikazee Planes, Blackhole, Etc.

  • Special Aimbot

    A Special Aimbot that improves on many things that the intergrated 2T1 Aimbot lacks.
    Include cool things like Magic Bullets, Bullet type, Deadzones, etc.

  • Gee-Map

    Gives an 'On-Screen Map' that acts as a replacment to the pause menu map. Has hotkeys such as zoom, Teleport, Mark waypoint, Orbital location, etc.
    (Way better than Pause-Map)

  • In-depth overlays

    Many useful overlays to keep you updated on alot of info related to your GTA session. Info such as player names, Modder statuses, Distance, Level, KD, Time in session, etc.

  • Compatibility

    Developed with compatibility in mind.
    Works well with other popular scripts like Rimurus Script, KeksMenu, MoistScriptNG, and even lesser known ones.
    Also comes with 30+ translations. Like English, Chinese, Japanese, Australian, Russian, and French.
